MSYS CJK bitmap font notice

msys-cjk-14-regular.bdf and msys-cjk-14-bold.bdf are offline-rendered bitmap
derivatives of the Simplified Chinese faces in NotoSansCJK-Regular.ttc and
NotoSansCJK-Bold.ttc. They contain ASCII, GB2312 and the additional Unicode
characters found in the MSYS translation catalogs at build time. The internal
derivative family is "msyscjk"; an X11 compatibility alias named
"Noto Sans CJK SC" lets existing MSYS Tk applications select it.
